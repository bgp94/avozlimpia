A Voz Limpia

    
A clean Voice










This presentation has sound. Scroll to continue...

    






















    
At the beginning of 2016, Luis Escudero, Eyal Chipkewicz and Pilar Aguilera organised the first night of what was to become 'A Voz Limpia' or in English, 'A Clean Voice'. Held once a month since, it aims to provide a space in which members of Australia's Latin American community can read poetry and sing in Spanish.
